Club Trophies

English Premier League

Of all teams that have graced the premier league since its conception in 1992, only one team has won it more than 5 times.

Manchester United currently boasts a record number of 13 premier league trophy in the competition's near 3 decade history.

UEFA Champions League

Arguably the most prestigious club competition trophy in the footballing world,

Manchester United currently holds 3 CL trophies, having won their most recent one in May 2008.

The FA Cup

One of the longest running club competitions in football history, the FA Cup was first won in 1871.

Since then, Manchester United have won this competition 12 times, a feat only bested by Arsenal who have won it 13 times.
